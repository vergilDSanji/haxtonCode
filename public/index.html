Fundmentals Of Cyber Security

Confidentiality

Protect sensitive data from unauthorized access using encryption, strong password policies, and access controls.

Integrity

Ensure data accuracy and prevent unauthorized alterations by using hashing, version control, and regular integrity checks.

Availability

Maintain access to systems and data through regular backups, system redundancy, and a disaster recovery plan.

Phishing Simulation Campaigns

Understand Phishing Threats

Learn about the different types of phishing attacks and how they can compromise your information. Recognize common signs and tactics used by attackers.

Simulate Phishing Attacks

Participate in simulated phishing campaigns to experience real-life scenarios. This helps identify vulnerabilities and enhances your ability to detect phishing attempts.
